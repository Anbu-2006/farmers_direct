The Problem We Solve

India's Agricultural Supply Chain is Broken

85% of India's farmers are small and marginal, yet they earn only a fraction of what consumers pay. Here's why.

🔗

Too Many Middlemen

Produce passes through 4–6 agents before reaching the consumer. Each takes a cut, leaving farmers with 30–40% of the final price.

🏷️

False Organic Labels

Consumers pay premium prices for "organic" produce that is often chemically treated or conventionally grown — no way to verify.

🗑️

Massive Food Waste

40% of harvested produce in India is wasted due to poor demand-supply matching, logistics delays, and lack of market access.
