What We Offer

Built for the Future
of Agriculture

Every feature is designed to solve a real problem faced by Indian farmers and consumers in today's broken supply chain.

Blockchain Traceability

Scan any product's QR code to trace its journey from seed to your table. Zero false organic labeling.

AI Price Suggestions

Our AI analyzes APMC Mandi data to suggest fair market prices — farmers decide, but they're never uninformed.

Escrow Payments

Consumer money is held safely until delivery is confirmed. Then 95% goes directly to the farmer instantly.

24-Hour Delivery

GPS-tracked, temperature-controlled logistics ensure produce reaches consumers within 24 hours of harvest.

Verified Organic

Multi-stakeholder certification combining government APIs, blockchain, and consumer reviews eliminates fraud.

Market Insights

Farmers get demand forecasts and harvest planning tools. Plan smarter, waste less, earn more.

Simple Process

How Farmer's Direct Works

01
🌾

Farmer Lists Produce

Farmer registers, uploads produce photos, sets quantity and price. AI suggests fair rates from live market data.

02
🛒

Consumer Orders & Tracks

Consumers browse nearby listings, place orders, and pay via Razorpay. Live GPS logistics tracking guarantees 24-hour freshness delivery.

03
💳

Instant Payment

On delivery confirmation, 95% goes directly to the farmer's UPI account. Platform retains 5% to stay sustainable.
